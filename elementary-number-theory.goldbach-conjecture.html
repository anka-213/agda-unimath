The Goldbach Conjecture

Goldbach-conjecture : UU lzero
Goldbach-conjecture =
  ( n : ℕ) → (le-ℕ 2 n) → (is-even-ℕ n) →
    Σ ℕ (λ p → (is-prime-ℕ p) × (Σ ℕ (λ q → (is-prime-ℕ q) × (add-ℕ p q ＝ n))))
