Idea

The cycle index series of a species F is a type family indexed by finite families of cyclic types. Note that a finite family of cyclic types C·µ¢ uniquely determines a permutation e on the disjoint union C := Œ£·µ¢ C·µ¢ of the underlying types of the C·µ¢. This permutation determines an action F e on F C. The cycle index series of F at the family C·µ¢ is the type Fix (F e) of fixed points of F e.
