Idea

An action of a higher group G on a type is just a type family over BG.

Definition

action-∞-Group :
  {l1 : Level} (l2 : Level) (G : ∞-Group l1) → UU (l1 ⊔ lsuc l2)
action-∞-Group l2 G = classifying-type-∞-Group G → UU l2
