Idea

In this file, we define the type of finite species. A finite species is just a map from 𝔽 to 𝔽.

Definition

finite-species : UU (lsuc lzero)
finite-species = 𝔽 → 𝔽

species-finite-species : finite-species → species lzero
species-finite-species F X = type-𝔽 X
