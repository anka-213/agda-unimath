Idea

An automorphism on a type A is an equivalence A ≃ A. We will just reuse the infrastructure of equivalences for automorphisms.

Definitions

The type of automorphisms on a type

Aut : {l1 : Level} → UU l1 → UU l1
Aut Y = Y ≃ Y
