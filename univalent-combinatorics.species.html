Idea

In this file, we define the type of species. A species is just a map from 𝔽 to a universe.

Definitions

Species

species : (l : Level) → UU (lsuc l)
species l = 𝔽 → UU l

Transport in species

tr-species :
  {l : Level} (F : species l) (X Y : 𝔽) → type-𝔽 X ≃ type-𝔽 Y → F X → F Y
tr-species F X Y e = tr F (eq-equiv-𝔽 X Y e)
