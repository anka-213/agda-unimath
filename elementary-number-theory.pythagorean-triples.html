Idea

A Pythagorean triple is a triple (a,b,c) of natural numbers such that a² + b² = c².

Definition

is-pythagorean-triple : ℕ → ℕ → ℕ → UU lzero
is-pythagorean-triple a b c = (add-ℕ (square-ℕ a) (square-ℕ b) ＝ square-ℕ c)
