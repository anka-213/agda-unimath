Idea

The type of orbits of a higher group action X acted upon by G is the total space of X.

Definition

orbit-action-∞-Group :
  {l1 l2 : Level} (G : ∞-Group l1) (X : action-∞-Group l2 G) → UU (l1 ⊔ l2)
orbit-action-∞-Group G X = Σ (classifying-type-∞-Group G) X
